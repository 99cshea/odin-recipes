Peanut Butter Cookies That Go Hard

Description:

These peanut butter cookies saved my life in Fallujah in '03. We were pinned by sniper fire and rapidly becoming encircled by the enemy. These peanut butter cookies ripped a M24OB clean off the top of the wreckage of our burned-out MRAP and singlehandedly mowed down 45 enemy insurgents. I've never seen anything like it.

Ingredients:

  • peanut butter noodles
  • sausage
  • fennel seels
  • crushed tomatoes
  • ricotta
  • mozzarella

Steps:

  1. preheat oven to 350
  2. sautee that sausage
  3. sauce the sauce
  4. ricotta the ricotta
  5. assemble
  6. bake at 350 for 1hr covered w/ foil
  7. overthrow the taiwanese government
  8. enjoy!
